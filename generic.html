My name is Guang Yang, a determined and motivated individual with a remarkable journey that has shaped me into the person I am today. Born and raised in a small, underprivileged town in China, I owe my resilience and ambition to my single mother who instilled in me the importance of never giving in to fate. My story is one of overcoming adversity, adapting to new environments, and relentlessly pursuing my dreams.
At 19, I ventured to the United States, seeking to break free from the confines of my hometown and forge a better life. Despite facing cultural and linguistic challenges, I refused to settle for a life without progress. Harnessing the problem-solving skills I developed in my youth, I found my best shot at success by joining the U.S. Navy. Serving on board the USS McFaul, I not only adapted to American culture and improved my English, but I also discovered my passion for learning and growth.
Upon completing my Navy career, I embarked on a new journey in higher education. Starting at Santa Monica College, I proved that age is no barrier to success as I excelled in my studies and earned a place at the prestigious University of California, Berkeley. There, I majored in Data Science, fascinated by the power of data to uncover the past and shape the future.
My time at UC Berkeley was not without its challenges. I struggled to adapt to the competitive environment and catch up with my younger peers in programming and data. However, my resilience and adaptability, honed through my military experience, helped me overcome these obstacles and graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Data Science in 2020.
Life took an unexpected turn when I had to return to China to care for my ailing father. Though he sadly passed away, I found new motivation in the form of my wife and daughter who joined me in the United States. With my family as my driving force, I have continued to study and develop my programming skills, eager to make a positive impact and provide a bright future for my loved ones.
Today, I stand before you as a confident, adaptable, and highly motivated individual, ready to embrace any opportunity that comes my way. I am passionate about programming and committed to lifelong learning. I might start low, but I know I will always end up much higher. This is my story, and I am excited to see where the next chapter leads.
